Project Overview

This exhibit combines historical context with an interactive Tableau dashboard to map the Doctrine and Covenants by date, place, recipient, and theme.

Chronology

Follow the revelation timeline.

Track how sections emerged across the formative years of the early Church and connect them to key moments of pressure, migration, and organization.

Geography

See where history happened.

Use map views to connect revelations to Kirtland, Missouri, and other important sites in the restoration narrative.

Themes

Filter by doctrinal focus.

Sort the dataset by stewardship, priesthood keys, opposition, governance, and other repeated topics in the curriculum.
